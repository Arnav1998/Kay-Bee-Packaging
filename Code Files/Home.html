About Us

Kay Bee Packaging is one of the nation's leading corrugated box manufacturer.

Established with the ideology to provide high quality packaging products at an affordable price, Kay Bee Packaging has made its mark across the nation. We peovide bulk quantities of corrugated sheets, paper and other packaging material, both for domestic and export purposes.
